Sunset Scout Notification Service Privacy Policy
This service is a private, non-commercial hobbyist application built solely to send daily weather and sunset updates to a single verified family member.

Sunset Scout Notification Service Onboarding & Call to Action
How to Sign Up:
This is a private, non-commercial application. Individuals opt into this service exclusively via a verbal, in-person request to the application administrator and by providing their mobile number.

What to Expect:
Upon joining, users will receive a single welcome text message confirming enrollment. Afterward, subscribers will receive exactly one (1) automated text message per day, sent approximately 15 minutes prior to the local sunset time, containing a summary of local weather conditions and sunset metrics.

Disclosures & Fees:
Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency is exactly 1 msg/day.

How to Opt-Out:
You can terminate this service at any time by replying with the keyword STOP to any text message received. For help information, reply HELP.

Mobile Information Non-Sharing Statement:
No mobile phone numbers, opt-in data, or personal information collected for this service will be shared, sold, or rented to any third parties or affiliates under any circumstances.

Message Frequency:
The subscriber will receive exactly one (1) automated text message per day, sent approximately 15 minutes prior to the local sunset time.

Disclosure:
Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to opt-out at any time.
